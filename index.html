About Us

Built on Farmer Trust,
Rooted in Rural India

AADHAR AGRO MALL is a trusted agriculture store focused on providing high-quality products for farmers and dairy farm owners in Maharashtra. Established with the goal of being the farmer's foundation, we have been serving the community since 2004.

We deal in cattle feed, cow and buffalo standing mats, and dried sweetcorn buying and selling. Our aim is to support farmers with reliable products, fair pricing, and honest service — every single day.

  • Premium quality cattle feed for healthy livestock
  • Durable cow & buffalo comfort mats
  • Fair dried sweetcorn trading — buying & selling
  • Open 7 days a week, 9 AM to 11 PM
